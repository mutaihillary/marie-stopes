Welcome to Marie Stopes kenya

Everyone deserves the right to choose. We have been helping women and men with their reproductive options for over 40 years. We provide NHS-funded as well as self-funded treatment through our network of local clinics all over the Kenya. Your health and wellbeing is our biggest priority. While you are in our care, we will always make sure you're treated with dignity, respect and that you feel supported. You can give us a call whenever you need us. Our booking and advice line is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week And we hope you will agree. Join us, kick back and relax in serene surroundings.
